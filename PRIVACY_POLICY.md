# Privacy Policy for Tessum

Effective date: March 8, 2026

Tessum ("the App") is a puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

## Summary

- Tessum does not require account creation or login.
- Tessum does not collect personal information.
- Tessum does not use third-party analytics SDKs in the current release.
- Tessum does not show ads in the current release.
- Tessum does not transmit gameplay or personal data to our servers in the current release.

## Information We Collect

In the current release, Tessum does not collect personal data such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Precise location
- Contacts
- Photos
- Identifiers used for tracking

## Data Stored on Your Device

Tessum stores gameplay-related information locally on your device to support normal app behavior (for example, puzzle progress, settings, and local game statistics). This local data is not used to identify you and is not transmitted to us in the current release.

## Third-Party Services

In the current release, Tessum does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or monetization SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Tessum is a general-audience game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Future Changes

If future versions add features such as ads, analytics, or in-app purchases, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or at the time those features are introduced.

## Your Choices

Because Tessum currently does not collect personal data, there is no account data to access, correct, or delete through an in-app process.

## Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed through the developer contact listed on the App Store product page.
